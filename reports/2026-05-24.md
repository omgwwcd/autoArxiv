## 1. DeltaBox: Scaling Stateful AI Agents with Millisecond-Level Sandbox Checkpoint/Rollback

**Topics:** AI Agent, Sandbox, Checkpoint/Rollback, Operating System
**Authors:** Yunpeng Dong, Jingkai He, Yuze Hou, Dong Du, Zhonghu Xu, Si Yu, Yubin Xia, Haibo Chen
**Venue / Year:** arXiv 2026
**Links:** [Abstract](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22781v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.22781v1.pdf)

### 1. 一句话结论

DeltaBox 通过基于变化的增量状态管理，将 AI 智能体的沙箱检查点/回滚延迟降低到毫秒级（14ms 和 5ms），相比现有方法提升了一个数量级。

### 2. 为什么重要

当前 LLM 智能体在树搜索和强化学习中需要高频状态探索，但现有沙箱的完整状态复制导致数百毫秒到数秒的延迟，严重限制了搜索深度和并行扩展。DeltaBox 解决了这一基础设施瓶颈，使得智能体能够在固定时间预算内探索更多节点，从而显著提升任务性能。

### 3. 研究问题

1. 如何实现 AI 智能体沙箱的毫秒级检查点/回滚？
2. 如何利用连续检查点之间的高度相似性来减少状态复制开销？

### 4. 背景与问题设定

LLM 驱动的 AI 智能体在复杂任务（如代码修复、网页导航）中需要高频状态探索，例如测试时树搜索和强化学习。这要求对沙箱的完整状态（包括文件系统和进程状态）进行快速检查点和回滚。现有方法（如 Docker 提交、Firecracker 快照、CRIU）通过复制整个状态来实现，导致每次 C/R 延迟在数百毫秒到数秒，严重限制了搜索深度和并行扩展。关键挑战在于：1) 检查点延迟高，现有系统优化侧重于恢复而非检查点；2) 文件系统状态缺乏高效的 C/R 机制。

### 5. 方法概览

- 任务 / 环境: AI 智能体沙箱环境，包含文件系统（如工作目录、安装的包）和进程状态（如内存、上下文、文件描述符）。
- 变量 / 干预设计: 提出 DeltaState 抽象，将文件系统和进程内存视为基于变化的、事务性的状态对。通过两个协同设计的 OS 机制实现：DeltaFS 基于 OverlayFS 实现运行时热层切换，将文件更新降级为写时复制；DeltaCR 使用增量转储和模板进程 fork 实现进程状态快速恢复。
- 评价指标: 检查点/回滚延迟（毫秒级）、写放大因子、SWE-bench 通过率、RL 训练吞吐量。
- 模型比较: 与 Git stash、Docker commit、Firecracker VM 快照、CRIU 等基线方法对比，DeltaBox 在 C/R 延迟上实现了一个数量级的降低（14ms 检查点，5ms 回滚）。
- 我的理解: DeltaBox 的核心创新在于利用 AI 智能体工作负载中连续检查点的高度相似性，只复制变化部分而非完整状态。DeltaFS 通过 OverlayFS 的层叠机制和运行时热层切换实现文件系统的增量快照，DeltaCR 通过增量转储和模板 fork 实现进程状态的快速恢复，两者协同保证了状态的一致性和原子性。

### 6. 关键发现

- 毫秒级检查点/回滚延迟: DeltaBox 实现了 14ms 的检查点延迟和 5ms 的回滚延迟，相比现有方法（如 Firecracker 的 200ms-2s 检查点、120-700ms 恢复）降低了一个数量级。
- 显著提升搜索效率: 在 SWE-bench MCTS 工作负载中，DeltaBox 将状态管理开销从轨迹时间的 47-77% 降低到 3-6%，使智能体能够探索更多搜索节点。
- 写放大与变化量成正比: DeltaBox 的写放大因子仅为 4KB（实际变化量），而现有方法（如 Docker commit）的写放大与工作目录大小或层大小成正比。

### 10. 对 Agent / Skill / Memory 的启发

- 对 Agent 系统: DeltaBox 使得智能体系统能够高效地执行树搜索和强化学习，无需担心状态管理开销，从而可以探索更深的搜索树和更大的并行度。
- 对 Skill: 智能体可以更频繁地尝试和回滚动作，从而学习更复杂的技能，如多步骤调试和依赖管理。
- 对 Memory: DeltaBox 的模板池和异步预热机制有效管理了内存使用，同时保证了快速恢复。
- 对评测: 评估基准可以更准确地反映智能体的真实能力，因为状态管理开销不再成为瓶颈。

### 11. 局限性

- DeltaBox 目前仅支持单进程的进程状态管理，对于多进程或分布式智能体场景可能需要扩展。
- 模板池的 LRU 淘汰策略在极端工作负载下可能导致回滚延迟退化到慢路径。

### 12. 我的看法 / 个人笔记

DeltaBox 通过巧妙的 OS 级抽象和机制设计，解决了 AI 智能体沙箱状态管理的核心瓶颈，其基于变化的增量思想具有通用性。未来工作可以探索将其扩展到多进程和分布式环境，以及与其他智能体框架（如 LangGraph）的深度集成。

### 14. 总结

DeltaBox 提出了一种新的 OS 级抽象 DeltaState，通过 DeltaFS 和 DeltaCR 两个协同机制实现了 AI 智能体沙箱的毫秒级检查点/回滚。DeltaFS 基于 OverlayFS 实现运行时热层切换，将文件更新降级为写时复制；DeltaCR 使用增量转储和模板进程 fork 实现进程状态快速恢复。评估表明，DeltaBox 在 SWE-bench 和 RL 微基准测试中将 C/R 延迟降低到 14ms 和 5ms，状态管理开销从 47-77% 降低到 3-6%，显著提升了智能体的搜索和训练效率。

## 2. Understanding Data Temporality Impact on Large Language Models Pre-training

**Topics:** LLM Pre-training, Data Ordering, Temporal Knowledge, Continual Learning
**Authors:** Pilchen Hippolyte, Fabre Romain, Signe Talla Franck, Perez Patrick, Grave Edouard
**Venue / Year:** ICML 2026
**Links:** [Abstract](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22769v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.22769v1.pdf) | [Code](https://github.com/kyutai-labs/kairos)

### 1. 一句话结论

Temporally ordered pre-training yields more up-to-date and temporally precise knowledge than shuffled pre-training, without degrading general language understanding.

### 2. 为什么重要

This work reveals that the common practice of shuffling pre-training data creates a temporal gap in LLM knowledge, and demonstrates a simple alternative—sequential training—that produces models with better factual freshness, which is crucial for applications requiring current information.

### 3. 研究问题

1. How does data ordering during pre-training affect the acquisition of time-sensitive factual knowledge in LLMs?
2. Can temporally ordered pre-training produce models with more up-to-date knowledge without sacrificing general language understanding?

### 4. 背景与问题设定

LLMs are typically trained on shuffled corpora, resulting in knowledge that is frozen at train time and poorly temporally grounded. Models often perform worse on questions about recent events compared to older ones, even within their training cutoff. The impact of data sampling order on the temporal distribution of knowledge is poorly understood.

### 5. 方法概览

- 任务 / 环境: Pre-training 6B-parameter Transformer decoder models on Common Crawl snapshots, evaluating on general language understanding (OLMES) and temporal factual knowledge (KairosQA, TAQA).
- 变量 / 干预设计: Compare two conditions: (1) shuffled pre-training (standard, globally shuffled data from 2020-2024), and (2) sequential pre-training (data processed in strict chronological order from 2018-2025). Both models trained on 2.5T tokens with identical architecture and hyperparameters. Note asymmetry: shuffled baseline covers 2020-2024, sequential covers 2018-2025, but authors argue older data (2018-2019) has lower quality and does not give advantage.
- 评价指标: OLMES score for general language understanding; cloze formulation (CF) accuracy and generative F1 score on KairosQA for temporal knowledge; relative F1 gain on yearly temporal knowledge; also evaluated on TAQA (Zhao et al., 2024) for external validation.
- 模型比较: Sequential 6B model vs. shuffled 6B baseline, plus comparisons with open-source models (Llama 3.1-8B, Gemma3-4B/12B, Qwen3-4B/8B/14B, OLMo3-7B) on KairosQA.
- 我的理解: The authors designed a controlled experiment to isolate the effect of data ordering by keeping all other factors (architecture, data source, token budget) identical. They also created a new benchmark, KairosQA, to specifically measure temporal alignment, which is a key methodological contribution. The asymmetry in data range is acknowledged and partially justified by quality analysis.

### 6. 关键发现

- Sequential pre-training matches shuffled on general language understanding: Final OLMES scores are comparable between sequential and shuffled models, showing that temporal ordering does not degrade general capabilities. However, learning trajectories differ: shuffled model leads initially (e.g., ~70 vs. ~65 at 500B tokens), sequential model catches up in the final third of training, eventually surpassing the baseline (~75 vs. ~74 at 2.5T tokens).
- Sequential pre-training yields more up-to-date temporal knowledge: On KairosQA, the sequential model shows a 16% relative F1 gain for 2023-2024 vs. 2020-2021, while shuffled models (including recent open-source ones) show negative gains (e.g., -39% for Llama3.1-8B, -11% for Gemma3-12B, -14% for Gemma3-4B, -19% for Qwen3-14B, -15% for Qwen3-8B, -12% for Qwen3-4B, -15% for OLMo3-7B), indicating a temporal delay.
- Shuffled pre-training peaks on older data, and intermediate checkpoints show consistent temporal bias: Shuffled models perform better on facts from earlier years, possibly due to increased repetition of older information. Intermediate checkpoint analysis (Fig. 9 in Appendix A.1) shows that shuffled checkpoints have nearly identical temporal performance across all training lengths (e.g., accuracy on 2020 facts ~0.55, on 2024 facts ~0.45), while sequential checkpoints improve over time (accuracy on 2024 facts increases from ~0.40 at 315B tokens to ~0.55 at 2.5T tokens).
- Sequential pre-training also improves performance on TAQA: The authors validated their findings on an external temporal benchmark (TAQA), showing consistent advantages for sequential training. For example, on TAQA, the sequential model achieves an F1 score of ~0.60 vs. ~0.55 for the shuffled baseline on 2023 facts.

### 7. 最重要的图

- 图来源: Figure 1: Yearly temporal knowledge with Kairos. Relative gains in F1 score on KairosQA between the 2020–2021 and 2023–2024 periods.
- 为什么重要: This figure succinctly demonstrates the core finding: sequential pre-training (Seq) is the only model with positive relative F1 gain (16%) for recent vs. older facts, while all shuffled models (including recent ones) show negative gains (ranging from -11% to -39%), highlighting the temporal delay caused by shuffling.

Positive values mean the model is better on recent facts; negative values mean it is better on older facts. The sequential model (Seq) is the only one with a positive gain (16%), while all others show negative gains, indicating they are less accurate on recent facts.

### 9. 次重要图

- 为什么也重要: Figure 3 shows that despite initial lag (e.g., ~65 vs. ~70 at 500B tokens), sequential pre-training eventually matches and slightly surpasses shuffled pre-training on general language understanding (OLMES score ~75 vs. ~74 at 2.5T tokens), proving that temporal ordering does not compromise final model capacity.

### 10. 对 Agent / Skill / Memory 的启发

- 对 Agent 系统: Agents relying on LLMs for current information would benefit from sequentially pre-trained models, as they provide more temporally accurate knowledge without additional fine-tuning.
- 对 Skill: Sequential pre-training could be combined with continual learning strategies to maintain up-to-date knowledge over time, potentially reducing the need for frequent retraining.
- 对 Memory: The findings suggest that data ordering affects how knowledge is stored and retrieved; sequential training may lead to better temporal organization of facts in model parameters.
- 对评测: Temporal evaluation benchmarks like KairosQA are essential for assessing model freshness; standard benchmarks may miss temporal biases.

### 11. 局限性

- The study only explores one model size (6B parameters); results may not generalize to larger models.
- The sequential model was trained on a wider time range (2018-2025) than the shuffled baseline (2020-2024), introducing a slight asymmetry. Authors argue older data is lower quality and does not give advantage, but this remains a confound.
- The analysis focuses on factual knowledge; impact on other capabilities like reasoning or creativity is not explored.
- The sequential model's initial lag on OLMES (e.g., ~65 vs. ~70 at 500B tokens) suggests potential efficiency concerns for mid-training phases.

### 12. 我的看法 / 个人笔记

This paper provides compelling evidence that a simple change in data ordering can significantly improve the temporal freshness of LLMs without sacrificing general performance. The creation of KairosQA is a valuable contribution for future research. However, the practical adoption of sequential pre-training may face challenges due to the need for temporally ordered data and potential computational overhead. Future work should explore scaling to larger models and integrating with continual learning.

### 13. 这篇工作引用的近期高影响力相关文章

- TAQA: Temporal Awareness Question Answering
  为什么重要：Provides a benchmark for evaluating temporal knowledge in LLMs, used by the authors as an additional evaluation dataset.
  核心贡献：Introduces a dataset of 9,000 time-sensitive questions from Wikipedia tables covering 2000 to 2023.
  与当前论文关系：The authors use TAQA to further validate their findings, showing that sequential pre-training also improves performance on this external benchmark (e.g., F1 score ~0.60 vs. ~0.55 on 2023 facts).

### 14. 总结

This paper investigates the impact of data ordering during LLM pre-training on temporal factual knowledge. By comparing a 6B-parameter model trained on temporally ordered Common Crawl snapshots against a standard shuffled baseline, the authors find that sequential pre-training yields models with more up-to-date and temporally precise knowledge, while matching shuffled models on general language understanding. They introduce KairosQA, a benchmark of over 7,000 temporally grounded questions (7,167 subject–relation pairs after filtering from 17 million raw triplets, with filtering based on temporal variation and subject popularity), and release code (https://github.com/kyutai-labs/kairos), checkpoints, and datasets (https://huggingface.co/collections/kyutai/kairos). Key results include: sequential model shows 16% relative F1 gain for recent vs. older facts, while shuffled models show negative gains (-11% to -39%); intermediate checkpoint analysis reveals shuffled models have consistent temporal bias across training lengths; and sequential training also improves performance on TAQA (e.g., F1 ~0.60 vs. ~0.55 on 2023 facts). The findings highlight the temporal bias introduced by shuffling and suggest that sequential training is a promising direction for building more temporally aware LLMs.

## 3. Superhuman Safe and Agile Racing through Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning

**Topics:** 多智能体强化学习, 无人机竞速, 安全协调, 联赛式自对弈, 置换不变注意力编码器
**Authors:** Ismail Geles, Leonard Bauersfeld, Markus Wulfmeier, Davide Scaramuzza
**Venue / Year:** Preprint 2026
**Links:** [Abstract](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22748v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.22748v1.pdf)

### 1. 一句话结论

通过联赛式自对弈多智能体强化学习，无人机在高速多机竞速中超越人类冠军，碰撞率降低50%，并零样本泛化至安全人机交互。

### 2. 为什么重要

该研究首次证明多智能体强化学习能在物理高速动态环境中实现超人类安全协调，为机器人共存提供了从孤立安全约束转向多智能体交互的新范式。

### 3. 研究问题

1. 多智能体强化学习能否在高速物理交互中实现安全与性能兼得？
2. 联赛式训练能否使策略泛化到未见过的对手数量和类型？
3. 置换不变注意力编码器对处理可变数量对手是否关键？

### 4. 背景与问题设定

现有自主系统在孤立或模拟中表现超人类，但在共享动态真实空间中脆弱，源于单智能体范式忽略其他智能体。无人机竞速作为高保真测试床，要求高速（>22 m/s）下处理气动干扰和策略博弈，现有方法要么仅优化单机圈速，要么局限于低速双机交互。多机场景下，策略需同时应对非平稳性、状态空间爆炸和物理碰撞风险。

### 5. 方法概览

- 任务 / 环境: 四旋翼无人机在75米Split-S赛道（7个门）上进行多机竞速，速度达22 m/s，加速度7 g，涉及气动下洗和狭窄通道博弈。硬件为220克3英寸四旋翼，推重比6.5。
- 变量 / 干预设计: 采用联赛式自对弈（league-play），智能体与来自过去检查点和替代训练策略（如单智能体、独立PPO）的多样化对手种群训练；使用基于Perceiver的置换不变注意力编码器处理可变数量对手观测（保持输入顺序不变性），并引入粒子下洗模型模拟气动交互。所有策略基于PPO+LSTM。
- 评价指标: 主要指标为平均完赛率（三圈21门完成百分比），辅以圈速、碰撞率（按门/墙/对手分类）和排名分布。
- 模型比较: 对比单智能体（无对手观测）、独立PPO（有对手观测但固定输入）、虚构自对弈（self-play，仅对历史检查点）、联赛式（含多样化对手池）及无Perceiver编码器的联赛消融。
- 我的理解: 联赛式训练通过暴露于多样对手策略，迫使智能体学习鲁棒的避碰和策略适应，而非过拟合单一对手模式；Perceiver编码器是关键，因其处理可变数量对手时保持置换不变性，避免固定输入顺序导致的性能崩溃（消融实验显示无编码器时门碰撞率反而高于对手碰撞）。

### 6. 关键发现

- 联赛策略在真实多机竞速中超越人类冠军: 在1v1和3v1人机对抗中，联赛策略完赛率100%和91.67%，远超人类冠军的53.33%和58.33%；圈速也更快（最佳首圈5.540 s vs 6.627 s）。人类在竞争压力下风险行为增加，而策略保持稳定安全。唯一一次人类获胜因两架自主无人机碰撞。无人类-AI碰撞在3v1中发生。
- 碰撞率降低50%且性能损失极小: 大规模模拟（64,000场4机比赛）中，联赛策略完赛率最高，平均圈速4.96 s仅比单智能体基线慢0.03 s，但碰撞率降低50%，且对手碰撞占主导（门/墙碰撞仅小部分）。单智能体策略最快但完赛率最低，因缺乏对手意识。
- 零样本泛化至更多对手和人类: 训练于最多4机，但策略可泛化至8机自对弈场景，完赛率保持稳定；单智能体策略在4机时首门碰撞概率达85%。独立PPO虽在自对弈中表现良好，但面对陌生对手时完赛率降至约60%。

## 5. Claw AI Lab: An Autonomous Multi-Agent Research Team

**Topics:** Multi-Agent Systems, Autonomous Research, AI Laboratory Infrastructure
**Authors:** Fan Wu, Cheng Chen, Zhenshan Tan, Taiyu Zhang, Xinzhen Xu, Yanyu Qian, Dingcheng Gao, Lanyun Zhu, Qi Zhu, Yi Tan, Deyi Ji, Guosheng Lin, Tianrun Chen, Deheng Ye, Fayao Liu
**Venue / Year:** arXiv 2026
**Links:** [Abstract](http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22662v1) | [PDF](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.22662v1.pdf) | [Code](https://github.com/Claw-AI-Lab/Claw-AI-Lab)

### 1. 一句话结论

Claw AI Lab 将自主研究从黑箱流水线转变为可交互、可监控、可回滚的多智能体实验室平台，通过 Claw-Code Harness 的沙盒执行、反伪造检查等机制显著提升实验完整性与结果可靠性。

### 2. 为什么重要

该工作重新定义了自主研究的范式：不再是单向的论文生成，而是构建一个可交互、可审计的 AI 实验室基础设施，使研究者能实时监控、干预和迭代研究过程，显著提升实验完成度和结果可信度。

### 3. 研究问题

1. 如何将自主研究从隐藏的提示到论文流水线转变为可交互、可监控的 AI 实验室？
2. 多智能体分层架构与跨层反馈机制能否提升研究流程的完整性和结果可靠性？

### 4. 背景与问题设定

现有自主研究系统（如 AutoResearchClaw）多为端到端流水线，缺乏可交互性、可监控性和可回滚能力，导致实验部分运行、中间输出难以检查、最终报告与执行结果不一致等常见失败模式。Claw AI Lab 旨在解决这些问题，将自主研究构建为可用的、交互的、可靠性感知的科学基础设施。

### 5. 方法概览

- 任务 / 环境: AI 研究任务，包括探索、多智能体讨论和复现三种模式，支持从想法到论文的完整流程。
- 变量 / 干预设计: 用户通过统一仪表盘实例化完整研究团队，自定义角色、协作流程，并支持实时监控、工件检查和一键回滚/恢复。
- 评价指标: 六维度评分：技术深度与可复现性、结构与章节流畅性、新颖性与贡献、清晰度与术语、逻辑论证、引用与证据支持。由两个 LLM 评审者（ChatGPT 5.4 Thinking 和 Gemini 3.1 Pro）在独立对话窗口中评分。
- 模型比较: Claw AI Lab 使用 GPT-5.4 作为主模型和编码模型，Gemini-3-Pro-Image-Preview 生成图表，Qwen3.5-Plus/Qwen-Plus 作为后备模型；基线系统 AutoResearchClaw 使用 GPT-5.4 作为主模型，Gemini-2.5-Pro-Flash-Image 作为图像模型，GPT-4o/GPT-4o-mini 作为后备模型。
- 我的理解: Claw AI Lab 的核心创新在于将研究过程分解为五个可交互的分层（想法、规划、编码、实验、写作），每层由专用智能体处理，并通过跨层反馈循环实现迭代优化。Claw-Code Harness 通过沙盒执行、时间预算控制、NaN/Inf 检测、冒烟测试和反伪造检查（检测虚假指标、占位代码或模拟实现）确保实验完整性和结果可追溯。

### 6. 关键发现

- Claw AI Lab 在论文质量上持续优于 AutoResearchClaw: 在三个研究主题上，Claw AI Lab 平均提升 +15.5 至 +16.5 分（满分100）；在复现主题上，平均分从 73.0/100 提升至 78.0/100，提升 5.0 分。两个 LLM 评审者（ChatGPT 5.4 Thinking 和 Gemini 3.1 Pro）均一致给出更高评分。
- Claw-Code Harness 提升实验完整性与结果可靠性: 通过沙盒执行、时间预算控制、NaN/Inf 检测、冒烟测试和反伪造检查，减少了部分运行和错误报告等常见失败模式。
- 跨层反馈机制防止错误累积: 实验层的结果可触发规划层更新，重复失败可回溯至想法层，实现持续改进。
